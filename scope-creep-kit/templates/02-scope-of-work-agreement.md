## 5. Revision & Change Process

### Revisions (within scope)

This agreement includes **[2] revision rounds** per deliverable. Revisions cover:
- Adjustments within the agreed scope (colors, layout, content placement)
- Corrections to errors made by the service provider

### Changes (outside scope — requires Change Order)

The following require a formal Change Order Request before work begins:
- New features, pages, or deliverables not listed in this agreement
- Significant redesigns that deviate from the approved mockups
- Additional revision rounds beyond the included [2]
- Work in the "Explicit Exclusions" section

**No work outside scope will begin without a signed Change Order.** Verbal requests, email requests, Slack messages, or "quick asks" do not constitute approved scope changes.

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with [14] days written notice. Upon termination:
- The client pays for all work completed up to the termination date
- The service provider delivers all work completed up to that date
- Both parties return any confidential materials

## 9. Intellectual Property

- Upon full payment, the client owns all final deliverables.
- The service provider retains ownership of working files, templates, tools, and methodologies used to create the deliverables.
- The service provider may display the final work in their portfolio unless the client requests otherwise in writing.
