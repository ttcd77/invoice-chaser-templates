## Revision Policy

This project includes **[2] rounds of revisions** on all deliverables.

**What counts as a revision:** Adjustments within the agreed scope — color changes, layout tweaks, content swaps within the same page structure.

**What counts as new scope (requires Change Order):** New pages, new features, rewriting from scratch, designing assets not in the original scope.

Additional revision rounds beyond the included [2] are billed at **[$X/hour or $Y flat per round]**.
